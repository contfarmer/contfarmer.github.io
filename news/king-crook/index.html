YouTuber NoLifeShaq 最近透過個人 Instagram 私訊了將近 30 位知名饒舌歌手，向他們詢問在他們心目中各自認為有史以來最偉大的饒舌歌手名單，其中饒舌傳奇 Eminem 便在受訪藝人之中。不過 Eminem 並未在 Instagram 上回覆，而是透過 Twitter 發文，Eminem 寫道：「對我來說，以下的歌手沒有特別的排序：「Lil Wayne、2pac、Royce da 5’9”、Jay-Z、Redman、Treach、Kool G.rap, Biggie 和 King Crook。」除了上述這 9 位饒舌歌手之外，第一組名單之後 Eminem 又再加上一則貼文，列入了：LL [cool j]、Nas、Joyner [lucas]、Kendrick [lamar]、[j] cole、Andre [3000]、Rakim、[big daddy] Kane 等人。

事實上這並非他第一次向眾人公開他的喜好名單，Eminem 上次他的單曲《Til I Collapse》中的歌詞便提及這個主題，歌詞寫道：「I got a list, here’s the order of my list that it’s in/It goes Reggie, Jay-Z, 2Pac and Biggie/André from OutKast, Jada, Kurupt, Nas and then me.」其中可見絕大多數都與這次相同，相信 Eminem 所言不假，是真的十分喜愛這些饒舌歌手。

“Eminem Reveals His Greatest Rappers of All-Time List”

YouTuber NoLifeShaq recently posted a video of himself DM’ing 30 rappers on Instagram to find out who they think is the greatest rapper of all time. Amongst the group that was asked, “Who is the greatest rapper of all time?” was Eminem, but the celebrated rapper did not respond in time for the video.

Responding on Twitter, Slim Shady revealed his greatest rapper of all-time list. “For me, in no particular order… Toss up between [lil] wayne, [2]pac, royce [da 5’9”], jay[-z], redman, treach, [kool] g. rap, biggie & king crook.”

Following the first set of name with another tweet, “Plus redman, LL [cool j], nas, joyner [lucas], kendrick [lamar], [j] cole, andre [3000], rakim, [big daddy] kane…”

The last time Eminem touched on the subject was on his single “‘Til I Collapse,” in which he raps, “I got a list, here’s the order of my list that it’s in/It goes Reggie, Jay-Z, 2Pac and Biggie/André from OutKast, Jada, Kurupt, Nas and then me.”

“Joyner Lucas Responds To Eminem Putting Him, 2Pac, Nas, J. Cole + Lil Wayne Into The Greatest Rapper Of All Time Debate”

East Coast rapper Joyner Lucas is really feeling the love. The hip-hop entertainer went online to react to rap god Eminem putting him and a few other lucky musicians into his “Greatest of All Time” debate.

Joyner x Em

Lucas went to Instagram Sunday with his shocked and humbled reaction. Joyner compared the co-sign to NBA icon Michael Jordan naming his all-time favorite ballers.

“🙏🏽 if you played ball and studied Michael Jordan as the greatest, and then Michael Jordan name drops you as one of the greatest ball players of all time. That’s how this feels to me. 🙏🏽❤️” -Joyner Lucas’ Instagram

High-Key Details

This week, Em went online and responded to a question about his all-time favorite rappers. In addition to Lucas, Slim Shady included Kendrick Lamar, Lil Wayne, Notorious B.I.G., Redman, JAY-Z and 2Pac.

“For me, in no particular order… Toss up between wayne, pac, royce, jay, redman, treach, g. rap, biggie & king crook….Plus redman, LL, nas, joyner, kendrick, cole, andre, rakim, kane…” -Eminem’s Twitter
