AC Milan v Cagliari live stream: Watch Serie A online

The final weekend of the 2019/20 Serie A season has AC Milan looking to end on a high against Cagliari on Saturday at 7:45pm.

Milan can rest easy going into the final game. Sixth place was secured in midweek, meaning the Rossoneri will be in next season’s Europa League, albeit starting from the second qualifying round.

The sixth-placed finish is testament to the work Stefano Piolo has done since replacing Marco Giampaolo at the San Siro, lifting them from 16th upon his arrival. In particular, post-lockdown, only Real Madrid and Atalanta have collected more points than AC Milan’s 27 across Europe’s top five leagues.

They face a Cagliari side who also have very little to play for. They are far away from the relegation battle, though a top-half finish is still a possibility thanks to their 2-0 win over champions Juventus.

However, history is not on their side. AC Milan have beaten Cagliari more times than any other Serie A side, winning 41 of their matches in a run that has also included 15 straight home wins.

Player to watch:

AC Milan have a handful of exciting young players among their ranks but once again it is the veteran Zlatan Ibrahimovic writing the headlines. The 38-year-old returned to Milan in January after the end of the MLS season but is already the club’s second-highest scorer of the season.

In Milan’s 4-1 win over Sampdoria, Ibrahimovic scored twice to take his tally to nine, behind only Ante Rebic among the Rossoneri squad. His performances in front of goal have seen Ibrahimovic move closer to penning a longer-term deal at Milan, having initially signed until the end of the 2019/20 campaign.

According to reports, Milan are ready to offer Ibrahimovic a two-year deal that would take him into his 40s. But that wouldn’t be a problem for the Swedish striker, who recently compared himself to the reverse-ageing fictional character Benjamin Button.

“People tell me I’m old and tired but I’m just getting warmed up,” he said after his brace against Sampdoria, which moved him to within one goal of double figures.

“I’m like Benjamin Button. I’ve always been young, never old.”

Should Ibrahimovic score this weekend, it would be 13th season since 2004/05 in which the Swedish striker has scored at least 10 goals. During that same timeframe, only Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo (both 14) have achieved the feat on more occasions.

“AC Milan vs. Cagliari”

AC Milan veteran Zlatan Ibrahimovic had a penalty saved but later scored as his side finished the Serie A season with a 3-0 win over Cagliari on Saturday.

The 38-year-old, possibly playing the final match of his second stint at the club, took his tally to 10 league goals since returning in January and became the oldest player to score 10 or more goals in a Serie A season.

The Swede’s contract ended with Saturday’s match although he could extend it into next season according to some Italian media reports.

AC Milan went ahead with an own goal by Ragnar Klavan in the 11th minute. Rafael Leao hit the post from a narrow angle but the ball bounced off the Estonian defender and into the net.

Leao also rattled the crossbar before Milan were awarded a penalty for handball by Sebastian Walukiewicz one minute before halftime. However, Ibrahimovic’s spot kick was saved by Alessio Cragno.

Ibrahimovic made amends when he scored with an emphatic finish from Samu Castillejo’s pass in the 55th minute, and Castillejo exchanged passes with Giacoma Bonaventura to score the third two minutes later.

Milan, unbeaten since the season restarted in June, finished sixth with 66 points and will play in the Europa League qualifying rounds next season.

AC Milan melanjut tren tak terkalahkannya sejak masa lockdown hingga pekan terakhir Serie A. Menghadapi Cagliari, Rossoneri menang telak 3-0.

Pekan ke-38 Liga Italia menampilkan laga Milan vs Cagliari di Stadion San Siro, Minggu (2/8/2020) dini hari WIB. Tim tuan rumah langsung mengambil kendali serangan sejak menit pertama.

Gol! Milan mencetak gol pembuka di menit ke-10. Diawali tembakan Rafael Leao, bola kemudian membentur Ragnar Klavan yang justru mengarahkan ke gawang sendiri. Skor 1-0 untuk Rossoneri.

Milan terus mengepung pertahanan Cagliari selepas mencetak gol pertama. Hingga memasuki menit ke-35, skuat asuhan Stefano Pioli itu mengendalikan permainan dengan penguasaan bola mencapai 65 persen.

Pada menit ke-43, Milan mendapat kans emas dari titik putih setelah Zlatan Ibrahimovic dilanggar Sebastian Walukiewicz di kotak terlarang. Sang Singa Swedia maju sebagai eksekutor penalti, tetapi bola tendangannya masih bisa digagalkan kiper Alessio Cragno.

Tendangan penalti Ibrahimovic yang gagal itu menjadi peluang emas terakhir yang didapatkan Milan hingga babak pertama berakhir. Il Diavolo Rosso menutup 45 menit awal dengan keunggulan 1-0.

Memasuki babak kedua, Milan tidak mengendurkan serangannya. Hasilnya, gol kedua tim tuan rumah tercipta di menit ke-55.

Kali ini Ibrahimovic mampu membayar tuntas kegagalannya mencetak gol dari titik putih dengan bola tendangan kerasnya yang merobek jala Cagliari, setelah menerima umpan matang dari Samuel Castillejo. Milan menjauh 2-0.

Lima menit berselang, giliran Castillejo yang mencatatkan namanya di papan skor. Giacomo Bonaventura memberikan operan kepada Castillejo yang diselesaikannya menjadi gol. Kedudukan berubah 3-0 untuk Milan.

Cagliari mencoba mengejar ketertinggalan dan berupaya mencetak gol pertamanya. Peluang emas tim tamu baru didapatkan menjelang pertandingan berakhir.

Ricardo Ladinetti melepaskan bola tendangan bebas ke kotak penalti Milan yang disambut sundulan Paolo Farago. Namun, bola tersebut bisa dihadang Gianluigi Donnarumma dan hanya berbuah tendangan sudut.

Cagliari gagal mencetak gol di sisa waktu yang ada. Laga tuntas dengan kemenangan telak Milan 3-0.

Dengan kemenangan ini, Milan finis di urutan keenam klasemen Liga Italia dengan 66 poin. Sementara itu, Cagliari menyelesaikan musim ini dengan duduk di posisi ke-13 dan mengoleksi 45 poin dari 38 laga.
