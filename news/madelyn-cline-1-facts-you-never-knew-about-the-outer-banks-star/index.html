Running short of things to watch?

Well, not if you have Netflix! The popular streaming service is proving even more invaluable as the lockdown continues, continuing to deliver new seasons of familiar favourites and great new titles too.

They’ve recently achieved monumental success with the unbelievably wild documentary series Tiger King, but there’s more binge-worthy entertainment where that came from thanks to Outer Banks.

Created by Josh Pate, Jonas Pate and Shannon Burke, this American teen drama premiered on Wednesday, April 15th 2020 and audiences were happy to make quick work of it, with some racing through in just a couple of days.

Boasting romance, adventure and shocks, the series tells the story of the “Pogues”, a group of friends looking to have fun who unexpectedly embark on a treasure hunt.

At a palatable ten episodes, it’s proven quite the hit and the cast certainly plays a significant role in its fortune…

Outer Banks’ Madelyn Cline: Previous roles

Madelyn Cline stars in Outer Banks as Sarah Cameron, appearing across all ten episodes.

The 22-year-old actress does a phenomenal job and cemented herself as a fan-favourite early on in the series. If you’re wondering where you may have seen her before, perhaps this will jog your memory…

According to IMDb, she first appeared on screens in a 2009 short called Milites Christi (she played Matilda). Then, fast forward to 2011 and she landed the part of Maya Smith in the film 23rd Psalm: Redemption.

Beyond that, she starred in such shorts as 2012’s Children of Wax (Ashlyn) and Bridge the Gap (Tanya) before arriving at roles in the likes of the 2016 film Savannah Sunrise (Willow), TV series’ Maid to Order (Jenny), The Originals (Jessica), Stranger Things (Tina) and Vice Principals (Taylor Watts).

Notably, she also landed more recent roles in 2018’s Boy Erased (Chloe) with Nicole Kidman and Lucas Hedges, along with the part of Olivia in 2019’s The Giant.

As for the future, you can expect to see her in Rebecca Eskreis’ drama film What Breaks the Ice (Emily) and Once Upon a Time in Staten Island (Sophia Larocca) starring Naomi Watts.

When they’re not channeling the Goonies in their pursuit of loot, these teens partake in summer flings, go boating, and indulge in a beer or two. Each character is said to be around 16, so the latter activity isn’t exactly above board. However, the actors who play them are well above legal drinking age. “How Old is the Cast of ‘Outer Banks’”

Outer Banks is Netflix’s latest YA series that has fans on the edge of their seats as a group of friends go on the ultimate treasure hunting adventure that could leave them living large. However, finding the treasure isn’t as easy as it seems and despite the fact that these group of young Pogues can basically get away with everything that they wants since the island looks down on them, they’re getting more attention than they ever did thanks to their new hunt. John B., Kie, JJ, and Pope might be totally young on the show, but that doesn’t mean that they’re also played by actual teens IRL.

Of course, that is the same with most teen TV shows nowadays. Since the days of Gossip Girl, Glee, and 90210, teens on television have been played by actors in their 20s. While this might have something to do with some of the scenes that these actors shoot, it probably has more to do with labor laws that would prevent them from working a certain amount of hours if they were minors. So, in order to complete production as quickly as possible, older cast members are hired to play teenagers.

But just how much older is the cast of Outer Banks? And what age are they trying to play on the show? Here’s everything you need to know about the real ages of the cast of Outer Banks.

Chase Stokes as John B.

On the show, John B. is a minor as evidenced by child services coming to put him into foster care due to him not having a guardian in his life. This means that Chase Stokes is about 10 years older than his character.

Netflix

Age: 22

This might not be her first Netflix series, but Madelyn is not that much older than her character, Sarah.

Madison is one of the youngest cast members, but she’s only one month older than her co-star Jonathan.
